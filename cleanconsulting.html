- clean consulting -

A sustainable student organization.

Clean Consulting is an organization at UCLA aiming to empower companies to understand and act on their connections with the natural world.

As the lead graphic designer, I create promotional material for our project teams and work on organization branding. I was asked to create a design for our team apparel.

Hand-Lettering

Something that most, if not all, of the board wanted to keep from the previous year's design was our organization's name hand-lettered. I wrote out our name using a few different hand-lettering styles to provide a range to choose from, that would best suit the design.

Sketching

Clean Consulting, at its core, is an organization for promotion of sustainable action to preserve nature. The design from the previous year had a lot going on, so I wanted to make it simplier. As such, the concept of nature led me to flora and botanical gardens.

Below are some sketches of various botanical life that I found appealing. These sketches were not done with the intent to put them on a shirt - rather, I wanted to explore the variety in plant life first, so I could develop a solid foundation in plant structure, as well as develop a specific style for drawing flora.

I then combined the hand-lettered name with some of the botanical life that I sketched out as well as found online, and came up with a very rough idea of what this shirt should look like. This design was to be on the back of the shirt, in a position similar to where the Patagonia logo is on their apparel.

I also wanted to maintain the simplicity across the front and backs of the shirt, so I decided on a basic left-breast flower design for the front.

Results

I used Affinity Designer and the vector pen tool to draw out the design. After a lot of adjustment, I reached a final product that I was pleased with - see below.
