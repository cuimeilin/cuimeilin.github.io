- clean consulting -

A sustainable student organization.

Clean Consulting is an organization at UCLA aiming to empower companies to understand and act on their connections with the natural world.

As the lead graphic designer, I create promotional material for our project teams and work on organization branding. I was given free range to create a design for our team apparel.

Hand-Lettering

We wanted to keep the hand-lettered aspect of last year's apparel, so I sketched out a few potential options that I thought would be suitable for a shirt.

Things of note:

  • loops had be large enough to prevent the printed design from being muddled
  • I decided on a continuous width throughout up- and down-strokes to be consistent with the uniformity of my botanical style (below)
  • ultimately, I decided on the top lettering style: letters were generally of uniform height and the stacked words allowed for substantial bordering detail

Sketching

Clean Consulting, at its core, is an organization for promotion of sustainable action to preserve nature. I wanted a realtively clean and simple design that was still "pretty" and ultimately decided on flora and botanical life to serve as decorative elements.

I made some sketches from life and from images references to explore the variations in plant life and to develop a foundation in plant structure as well as a specific style in drawing flora.

I then combined the hand-lettered name with some of the botanical life that I sketched out as well as found online, and came up with a very rough idea of what this shirt should look like. This design was to be on the back of the shirt, in a position similar to where the Patagonia logo is on their apparel.

(Tentative) Results

I used Affinity Designer and the vector pen tool to draw out the design and will be increasing the spacing between some smaller line work to ensure everything is printed clearly.
